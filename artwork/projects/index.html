Il Santo Choir Screen Reconstruction

Series of illustrated reconstructions with digital labeling for a Renaissance choir screen at Il Santo in Padua, Italy. Based on historical descriptions of the original choir screen, mathematical calculations, photographs from the early 1900's, and surviving portions of the furnishing.

Neuropsychology Honors Thesis Poster

Senior honors thesis project displayed at public poster session summary the objective, procedure, results, and analysis of findings. Hypothesis: “Based on anatomical data that hippocampal CA2 receives convergent information coding spatial and non-spatial information, we hypothesize that learning the association between objects and locations will result in defined areas of neuronal plasticity as revealed by Arc expression.” This research won the Charles Flaherty Award in Undergraduate Research.
